At long last, at the end of your journey, you arrive back in your quaint little village.
It has been a long time, since you set out on your journey to become more worldly, but you can say for sure you are still not like your master.
You're outside of his permanent room in the tavern, and you knock on his door.
No one answers.
You try the handle, and it is locked.
You think that it is fine to wake him, since it has been a long time and that he'll be glad to see you again, so you produce a spare key that he gave you many years ago.
You open the door, and rather then see him in bed, he's in the chair, looking outside.
Master! I have returned.
He turns to you, and a smile grows on his face.
"Ah, apprentice! Or rather simply another traveling bard now eh?", He smiles at you.
"Let us head down, and show me what you've learned!"
